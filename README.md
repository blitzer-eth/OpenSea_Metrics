This dashboard provides a deep-dive technical and financial analysis of **OpenSea's** cross-chain trading activity, revenue performance, and user engagement. It aggregates raw on-chain data from Ethereum, Solana, and 8 other EVM chains to transform complex transaction logs into actionable business intelligence.

The dashboard is designed to track OpenSea‚Äôs evolution from a single-market NFT platform into a multi-asset, multi-chain trading powerhouse using four core analytical modules.

## üîç Query Breakdown

### 1. OpenSea Annualized Revenue:

* Projects yearly earnings by calculating 30-day rolling platform fees across all supported chains.

### 2. OpenSea Cumulative:

* Tracks the lifetime aggregate of total volume and platform fees generated since the marketplace's inception.

### 3. OpenSea Daily Volume:

* Monitors 24-hour trading throughput, broken down by asset type (Tokens vs. NFTs) and source chain.

### 4. OpenSea Monthly Revenue:

* Provides a high-level financial summary of platform earnings on a month-over-month basis.

### 5. OpenSea Monthly Volume:

* Analyzes long-term trading trends and market share shifts across the broader NFT and Token ecosystem.

### 6. OpenSea daily active users:

* Identifies unique daily participants by merging buyer and seller data while filtering out wash trading.

### 7. OpenSea monthly NFTs sold:

* Quantifies the total inventory movement by counting unique ERC-721 and ERC-1155 transfers.

### 8. OpenSea monthly active users:

* Measures unique monthly user retention and ecosystem growth over a multi-year timeframe.
